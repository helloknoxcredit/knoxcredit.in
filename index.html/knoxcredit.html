Who Can Apply?

Basic eligibility criteria for most loan products through KnoxCredit.

For Salaried Individuals

✅ Age: 21 to 60 years
✅ Minimum salary ₹15,000/month
✅ At least 6 months in current job
✅ CIBIL score 650+ preferred
✅ Valid bank account & KYC documents

For Self-Employed / Business

✅ Age: 25 to 65 years
✅ Business operational for 2+ years
✅ Annual income ₹2L+ (ITR proof)
✅ CIBIL score 650+ preferred
✅ GST registration (for business loans)
